What We Believe In

In a world where technology and healthcare are increasingly intertwined, the security of medical devices and the protection of patient data are more critical than ever. Imagine a pacemaker being hacked, a hospital’s entire database held ransom, or a life-saving device failing due to outdated software. These are not hypothetical scenarios—they are real threats that we face today. With every challenge comes an opportunity for innovative solutions for VivaSecuris to support you.

Finally, the industry is continuously improving its defenses. Artificial intelligence and machine learning are being deployed to automate threat detection and response, while blockchain technology is enhancing data integrity and traceability. The healthcare sector is also putting an emphasis on regular risk assessments and staying updated on the latest security threats and technologies. As we move forward, these continual improvements will play a crucial role in ensuring the security of our healthcare system.

Regular risk assessments are essential to proactively protect against cyber threats.
The emergence of transformative technologies across the healthcare spectrum further compounds the need for vigilant cybersecurity measures.
The U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) recently alerted healthcare providers about a cybersecurity vulnerability affecting software in certain medical devices. This incident underscores the critical need for constant vigilance and the importance of addressing vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.
In an alarming development, hackers have begun targeting medical devices that provide critical care. A recent attack on software cancer provider Elekta's infrastructure demonstrated the potential for hackers to infiltrate these systems. This incident serves as a stark reminder of the tangible risks posed by inadequate cybersecurity, emphasizing the need for robust protection of medical devices to ensure uninterrupted and safe patient care.
