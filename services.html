Our Services

Vulnerability Management

Comprehensive plans and tools for monitoring, identifying, and addressing postmarket cybersecurity vulnerabilities and exploits in medical devices.

Secure Design and Development

Consulting services and tools to help medical device manufacturers design, develop, and maintain cybersecure devices and related systems.

Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) Solutions

Software to generate and manage SBOMs for medical devices, including commercial, open-source, and off-the-shelf components.

Postmarket Update and Patch Management

Systems to facilitate the distribution and implementation of postmarket updates and patches for medical devices.

Regulatory Compliance Support

Services to help manufacturers meet FDA cybersecurity requirements in their premarket submissions (510(k), PMA, PDP, De Novo, HDE).

AI-Specific Cybersecurity

Specialized solutions to address unique cybersecurity challenges posed by AI-enabled medical devices.

AI Products and AI Safety Specialization

AI-Specific Risk Assessment

Tailored risk assessment methodologies for AI-enabled medical devices, considering both cybersecurity and AI-specific risks.

AI Model Validation

Tools and services to validate AI models used in medical devices, ensuring they perform safely and as intended across diverse patient populations.

AI Update Management

Specialized solutions for securely updating AI models in deployed medical devices, maintaining cybersecurity while improving AI performance.

AI Ethics and Bias Mitigation

Tools and consultancy services to identify and mitigate potential biases in AI algorithms used in medical devices, promoting fair and equitable healthcare outcomes.
